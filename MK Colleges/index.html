About Us

A Legacy of Academic Excellence

Founded in 1978, MK Institution has grown into one of India's most respected multidisciplinary universities, with NAAC A++ accreditation and a vibrant community of 15,000+ students.

We offer programmes across Engineering, Arts, Medical Sciences, Commerce, and Science — all taught by 700+ distinguished faculty members.
